Welcome to Darjeeling

Darjeeling is famous throughout the world for the tea it grows and the great view of the Kanchenjunga range of the mountains that it offers. It is also know for it's richness in culture and in nature heritage and the famous toy train that has been declared a UN heritage.

These are some places you must visit when you go to darjeeling

  • Observatory hill view

    Observatory Hill is one of the best spots from where you can get a panoramic view of the resplendent natural beauty of Darjeeling.Views of snow-clad peaks, including Mount Kanchenjunga, are visible from the Observatory Hill.

  • Tiger hill

    The Darjeeling Tiger Hill attracts tourists worldwide towards Darjeeling for the panoramic views of snow capped mountains of Himalayas.It has a panoramic view of Mount Everest and Mount Kanchenjunga together.

  • Singalia National park

    Singalila National Park is a National park of India located on the Singalila Ridge at an elevation of more than 2300 metres above sea level, in the Darjeeling district of West Bengal.[1] It is well known for the trekking route to Sandakphu that runs through it.

  • Happy Valley Tea Estate

    Happy Valley Tea Estate is a tea garden in Darjeeling district in the Indian state of West Bengal. Established in 1854, it is Darjeeling's second oldest tea estate. Spread over 177 hectares (440 acres), it is situated at a height of 2,100 metres (6,900 ft) above sea level.

  • Batasia Loop

    Batasia Loop attracts people for spectacular view of Mt. Kanchenjunga & other snow capped Himalayan Peaks and watch the remarkable engineering marvel of Darjeeling Himalayan.At the centre of Batasia Loop a War Memorial stands.
