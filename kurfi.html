Welcome to Kufri

Kufri is a small hill station located in the shimla district. The scenic view and snow-capped landscapes atrract tourists from all part of the country and abroad.

These are some places you must visit when you go to kufri

  • Green Valley

    The Green Valley is a beautiful and breathtaking mountain range that falls on the way to Kufri from Shimla. The Green Valley is surrounded by verdant hills on all sides covered with thick pine and deodar forests.

  • Mahasu Peak

    Mahasu Peak is the highest peak in Kufri of Himachal Pradesh, ranging at an altitude of 2,720 meters. The views from the peak are breath taking, one can view the ranges of Kedarnath and Badrinath from this peak. This place is also popularly known for adventures like trekking, skiing, hiking.

  • Adventure resort

    The Kufri adventure park is one of the finest Hill Adventure Sports experience ever in North India at 9000 ft height from Sea Level.

  • Fagu

    Situated at an elevation of 2500 meters, Fagu is a serene snow-clad hamlet, located around 45 minutes drive from Shimla. This place looks magical as it is always enveloped in snow and fog.
